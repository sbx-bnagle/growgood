# Custom Garden Design

<p class='deck'>
	We work with homeowners and renters across Chicago and the upper midwest to design native perennial gardens that are beautiful, ecologically sound, and built to last. Every project is different, and we're happy to talk through yours before committing to anything.
</p>

## Our Process

### Step 1: Initial Conversation
We start with a conversation about your space, your goals, and your budget. This can happen over email, phone, or in person — whatever works best for you.

#### You Receive
- Shared understanding of scope, goals, and budget
- A sense of whether the project is a good fit
- Outline of next steps, typically a scheduled site visit

<br>

### Step 2: Site Visit
We come out to take a look at your space in person, whether that's a hell strip, a shady side yard, or a full backyard. We assess soil conditions, light exposure, and drainage so we can make plant recommendations that are suited to your specific site rather than just your zip code.

#### You Receive
- Written notes on soil conditions, light exposure, and drainage
- Site measurements and photographs
- Initial recommendations or observations shared verbally or in a follow-up email

<br>

### Step 3: Garden Plan
We put together a planting plan that shows exactly what goes where. You'll receive a scaled layout, a full plant list, and notes on installation and long-term care.

#### You Receive
- Scaled planting plan showing plant placement
- Full plant list with names, quantities, and brief descriptions
- Installation notes and sequence
- Long-term maintenance guide

<br>

### Step 4: Plants, Sourced and Delivered
We source your plants and deliver them to your door, clearly labeled and ready to go in the ground. We work with trusted local and regional nurseries to supply healthy, climate-adapted native perennials.

#### You Receive
- Plants delivered to the door, clearly labeled by name and placement
- Delivery schedule confirmed in advance
- Any substitutions noted if original selections are unavailable

<br>

### Step 5: Installation Support
We don't install gardens ourselves, but we'll connect you with trusted local contractors if you'd prefer not to do it yourself. If you're installing on your own, your garden plan includes everything you need to do it with confidence.

#### You Receive
- Contractor referrals if requested
- Written installation instructions included with the garden plan
- Availability to answer questions during installation

<br>

### Step 6: Ongoing Support
Once your garden is in the ground, we're still available. We're happy to answer questions as they come up and can return for a follow-up site visit if your garden needs attention down the road.

#### You Receive
- Email or phone availability for follow-up questions
- Optional return site visit
- Seasonal care reminders if offered
