Our kits bring together native perennial plants designed for beauty, function, and your local habitat. Every kit delivers real color and texture alongside real benefits for birds, bugs, and the community that grows around them.

Kits are sold by the square foot and scale from a small patio to a full backyard. Each includes:

  • a garden plan tailored to your site conditions,
  • plants ready to go in the ground,
  • installation instructions,
  • and a long-term maintenance guide.

Low-maintenance and complete on their own, our kits also serve as a foundation to expand and make your own over time. Plant selection rotates with the season, but every kit is designed to perform.
