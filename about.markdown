# About Grow Good Gardens


## We Make Gardens Accessible. 

Perennial plants can be expensive and difficult to find. And the information available is often hyper-local or frustratingly vague. Despite that, gardening can be easy and enjoyable. A comfortable garden is a place to connect with friends, family, and neighbors. We want to help you create one.


### Affordable Gardens for Small Spaces
You shouldn&rsquo;t have to drop a ton of cash or have acres of sunshine to create a beautiful garden. We specialize in small spaces and tight budgets, with a focus on plants that thrive in the upper midwest climate. Shady patios and crowded parkways are just as worthy as spacious yards. You can save money by installing the garden yourself. We&rsquo;re also happy to point you toward a trusted contractor.


### Sustainable Garden Design
We&rsquo;re into gardens that sustain and restore their surroundings. We emphasize native plants that grow naturally in your area. They provide food and habitat for birds, butterflies, and other local critters. And once established, they conserve water rather than consume it. A responsible garden is easier to maintain and fuller of life. A win-win 😎.


### Low-Maintenance Native Plants
With the right plants in the right place, a garden largely takes care of itself. Native perennial plants make for low-maintenance gardens without constant weeding and watering. We select plants suited to your space so they can thrive without being babied. We&rsquo;ll provide detailed instructions to care for your garden and we&rsquo;re always happy to answer questions as they arise.


### Custom Garden Design for Every Style
Grow Good Gardens is here to express your tastes, not ours. Whether you prefer modern, traditional, minimal, or natural gardens, we have the expertise to plan a space that feels like you. Our kits provide a solid foundation to build from, or we can create a custom design tailored to your preferences. We love to talk garden. Don't hesitate to reach out.
