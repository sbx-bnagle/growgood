# Contractor Referrals

<p class='deck'>
	Contractors we trust for the work we don't do.
</p>


We focus on design and plants. For the rest, we gotta guy. Every contractor we refer has been vetted for quality, reliability, and an approach to landscaping that aligns with ours.

We can connect you with contractors for:

- Garden installation
- Hardscaping and stonework
- Drainage and grading
- Ongoing garden maintenance, particularly for clients outside our service area

<br>

## A note on installation

We think there's real value in installing your own garden. It saves money, and the process of planting, getting your hands in the soil, and connecting with your space is part of what makes a garden worth having. Every kit and custom plan comes with detailed instructions to help you do it with confidence. That said, we understand it's not always practical. If you'd prefer to hand off the installation, we're happy to connect you with someone we trust.


## Prefer to hand it off completely?

If you&rsquo;d rather not coordinate contractors yourself, we can manage that for you. We'll handle the referrals, coordinate the work, and make sure the installation follows the plan. All you need to do is enjoy the result.
